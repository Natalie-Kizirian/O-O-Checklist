Important Guidlines

  • Inspection Duration: An apartment inspection should take between 15-25 minutes ,depending on the size. If it takes less, it means the check was not thorough enough.

  • Submission: you send a photo of the completed checklist to the cleaning group.

  • Reporting: Include comments and photos if anything was found during the inspection to ensure it doesn't happen again.

    • General & Living Area

    Water Heater (Boiler): Ensure the water heater is switched OFF.
    TV Settings: Ensure the TV interface/language is set to English.
    Remote Controls: Check that all remotes (TV & AC) are working (check batteries).
    Climate Control: Set the AC/Heating temperature according to the current season.
    Remotes Placement: TV and AC remotes in each room on the side table near the sofa or bed.
    Internet: Check Internet speed to ensure Wi-Fi is working at full capacity.
    Walls Inspection: Check all apartment walls for dampness, moisture or peeling paint.
    Sofa Bed (If appicable): Sofa is prepared for guest, cushions are in the basket or bedroom.
    Balcony: Floor swept, railings wiped and drainage clean and free of leaves/debris
    No Smoking Evidence: Check for cigarette butts, ash or smells.
    Floors: 100% free of dust, hair and debris (especially in corners).
    Dust Inspection: Check above the refrigerator, behind the TV, above the wardrobe, and the toilet vent for accumulated dust.

    Bedroom

    Bed Linens: Freshly laundered, pulled tight, and 100% hair-free (use a lint roller)
    Baby Crib (If applicalble): Crib with linens, a small towel, verify the mattress is inside.
    Under the Bed: Completely clean with zero dust, hair or leftover items.
    Storage: Closets and drawers are empty and wiped clean.

    Kitchen

    Kettle: Descale and ensure the inside of the kettle is 100% free of limescale.
    Appliances: Deep clean the inside of the oven and microwave (where applicalble)
    Fridge: Deep clean inside and out (no food residues or stains).
    Cabinets: All dishes and cutlery inside are 100% clean, dry and organized.
    Countertops: Wiped clean with zero crumbs, grease or dust.
    Sink: Dry and polished with no food residue or water spots.

    Bathroom

    Shower Drain: Check inside the drain. It must be 100% free of hair/clogs.
    Towel Placement: Towels must be in the bathroom (not on the bed) if space allows.
    The "No Hair Rule": Double-check the shower floor, sink and toilet for stray hairs.
    Silicones & Grout: Ensure all silicones are white without mold or black spots. Check that ceramic grooves (grout) are not yellow or missing.
    Shower Fixtures: Check the shower head and pipe for mold, stains or scale.
    Shower Strips: Verify the silicone plastic strips are not worn out or yellowed.
    Toilet: Sanitized and spotless (check the base and behind the seat).
